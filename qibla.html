📚 About Qibla
The Qibla (قِبْلَة) is the direction toward the Kaaba in Makkah — the direction Muslims face during Salah.

Kaaba: 21.4225° N, 39.8262° E

When your phone has a compass sensor and you allow location, the needle updates in real-time as you rotate your phone.
